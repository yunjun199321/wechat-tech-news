**Tech news specifics:**
- Weekly roundups: Friday 18:00 or Sunday 10:00
- Breaking news: Within 2 hours of event
- Analysis pieces: Wednesday evening

### Content Frequency

**Optimal**:
- Weekly roundups: 1x per week
- Breaking news: As needed (max 1 per day)
- Deep dives: 1x per 2 weeks

**Avoid**:
- More than 1 article per day (reduces per-article engagement)
- Less than 1 article per week (audience attention fades)
