## Specific Topic Guidance

### AI and Technology

**Safe to cover:**
- Technical breakthroughs
- Product launches
- Research findings
- Industry partnerships

**Requires care:**
- Dual-use technologies
- Government contracts
- National security applications
- Cross-border data flows

### Finance and Investment

**Safe to cover:**
- Funding rounds (with disclaimers)
- Company valuations (with sources)
- Market trends (with analysis)
- IPO news (factual reporting)

**Requires care:**
- Stock price predictions
- Investment recommendations
- Market crash narratives
- Insider trading allegations

### Policy and Regulation

**Safe to cover:**
- Published regulations
- Industry standards
- Compliance requirements
- Official announcements

**Requires care:**
- Policy speculation
- Regulatory criticism
- International disputes
- Enforcement actions

## Emergency Response

### If Content is Flagged

1. **Immediate Actions**
   - Unpublish if possible
   - Review flagged sections
   - Identify violation category

2. **Remediation Steps**
   - Apply appropriate language optimization
   - Add missing disclaimers
   - Rebalance content if needed
   - Seek editorial review

3. **Republication Process**
   - Wait for clearance
   - Re-run compliance checklist
   - Monitor initial response
   - Be prepared to adjust further
